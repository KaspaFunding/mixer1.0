                How to Participate in Zero-Trust Coinjoin (Quick Guide)
              

              ‚ñº
            

            

              

                
Step 1: Create Your Session

                
    
                  
  • Click "Create Zero-Trust Session" button
    • 
                  
  • Enter your destination address (where you want to receive mixed funds)
    • 
                  
  • Click "‚ú® Use My Wallet" to auto-fill from your imported wallet, or manually enter UTXOs
    • 
                  
  • Click "Create Session" - Your UTXOs are now committed (hashed)
    • 
                

                
                
Step 2: Reveal Your UTXOs

                
    
                  
  • Once your session shows "committed" status, click "‚ú® One-Click Reveal" (if available)
    • 
                  
  • Or click "Reveal UTXOs" to manually enter your UTXO details
    • 
                  
  • Your session status will change to "revealed"
    • 
                

                
                
Step 3: Wait for Other Participants

                
    
                  
  • You need 3-10 participants (including yourself) to build a coinjoin transaction
    • 
                  
  • Check the "Participants: X of 3-10" indicator on revealed sessions
    • 
                  
  • When it shows "‚úì Ready to Build!", you can proceed
    • 
                

                
                
Step 4: Build Transaction

                
    
                  
  • Click the "üî® Build Transaction" button on any revealed session
    • 
                  
  • The system will create a transaction structure with equal outputs for all participants
    • 
                  
  • Note: Each participant must sign their own inputs - this is a zero-trust system
    • 
                

                
                
Step 5: Sign Your Inputs

                
    
                  
  • When the transaction is built, click "‚úçÔ∏è Sign My Inputs" button
    • 
                  
  • Your private key is used locally on your device to sign - it never leaves your computer
    • 
                  
  • Wait for all participants to sign their inputs
    • 
                

                
                
Step 6: Submit Transaction

                
    
                  
  • Once all participants have signed, click "Submit Transaction"
    • 
                  
  • The transaction will be broadcast to the Kaspa network
    • 
                  
  • Your mixed coins will arrive at your destination address
    • 
                

                
                

                  üîí Security Note: Your private keys are never transmitted to the server. They stay on your device and are only used locally for signing your inputs. This is a true zero-trust system.
                

                
                

                  üí° Tip: The participant count updates automatically. Refresh the page or click "Refresh" to see the latest count.
